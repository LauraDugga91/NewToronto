ISA Certified Arborists

Our arborists are ISA Certified, meaning that they can accurately asses and diagnose trees legally within the City of Toronto. Our arborists service Toronto, Etobicoke, Scarborough, Mississauga, North York, and other areas.

Services

Tree Removal

New Toronto Tree Care specializes in complicated tree removal. Our years of experience and top-of-the-line equipment ensures that we get the job done. Our arborists use a complicated system of roping and rigging to make sure that your tree comes down safely and efficiently.

Pruning and Trimming

Pruning and trimming your tree can not only improve the aesthetics of your entire property, but proper maintenance of your tree will improve its overall health for years to come. Our arborists specialize in preventative pruning to ensure that your tree is safe in the case of any unexpected weather or storms.

Stump Grinding

After your tree is removed, you could be left with an unsightly stump that comes above ground level. The only way to ensure that there will be no re-growth of the tree is to grind the stump down below ground level. This helps to kill the roots, and allows you to plant or cover the area in grass.

Arborist Reports and Consultations

In order to remove a tree on your property larger than 30cm in diameter, you'll need to submit an Arborist Report to the city for approval. One of our ISA Certified Arborists will assess the tree and prepare an official document for Urban Forestry to review. Are you concerned about a possible disease or infestation on your property? Our arborists can meet with you for a scheduled consultation to assess and diagnose any problems with your tree, and they will also be able to recommend possible treatment plans. Ask us about our Emerald Ash Borer specialists.

Fertilizing and Spraying

We offer a wide range of fertilizers and sprays to help combat diseases and insect infestations. Fertilization should also be considered as a preventative measure. Our arborists will be able to advise which products to use, as well as the best time to treat your tree.
